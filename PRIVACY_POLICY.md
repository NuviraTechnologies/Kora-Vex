# Kora Vex Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 31, 2026

## Overview
Kora Vex is an AI companion app that uses voice, text, and image analysis to provide intelligent conversation and entertainment. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

## Data Collection

### Voice & Audio
- Microphone access is used to record your voice for transcription
- Audio is sent to our backend for processing and is not permanently stored
- Voice transcriptions are used to power the chat feature

### Photos & Camera
- Camera and photo library access allows you to share images with Kora Vex
- Images are analyzed by our AI and are not permanently stored
- Images are deleted after analysis

### Chat History
- Your conversations with Kora Vex are stored locally on your device
- Chat history is stored in the app's local database for context and memory
- You can delete your chat history at any time in Settings

### Device Information
- We collect basic device information for app performance and crash reporting
- This includes device model, OS version, and app version

## Data Usage
- Your data is used solely to provide the Kora Vex experience
- We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties
- We do not use your data for advertising or marketing

## Data Storage & Security
- Chat data is stored locally on your device
- Temporary files (audio, images) are encrypted in transit
- We use industry-standard security practices to protect your data

## Your Rights
- You can delete your chat history at any time
- You can revoke microphone, camera, or photo permissions in Settings
- You can contact us for data deletion requests

## Contact
For privacy questions, contact: support@nuviratechnologies.io

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted in the app.
